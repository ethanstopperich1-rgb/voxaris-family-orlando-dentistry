## Services

### Preventative Dentistry
Regular cleanings, exams, digital X-rays, fluoride treatments, and sealants.

### Cosmetic Dentistry
Professional teeth whitening, porcelain veneers, and smile makeover consultations.

### Invisalign Clear Aligners
The practice offers Invisalign treatment for crowding, spacing, and bite alignment issues. A free Invisalign consultation is currently available for new patients. The consultation includes a digital scan and visual preview of potential results. Treatment length varies but typically ranges from 6 to 18 months depending on complexity.

### Dental Implants
Full dental implant services including single-tooth implants, implant-supported bridges, and related bone grafting procedures.

### Same-Day Crowns (CEREC)
Single-visit crown technology using CEREC. The crown is designed, milled, and placed in one appointment — no temporary crown or second visit needed.

### Restorative Dentistry
Crowns, bridges, fillings, inlays, onlays, and full-mouth rehabilitation.

### Emergency Dental Care
The practice provides emergency services for cracked teeth, severe toothaches, trauma, abscesses, and related urgent conditions. Same-day emergency evaluation is available when the office is open. Root canal therapy and emergency extractions are performed on-site.

### Oral Surgery
Tooth extractions, including wisdom teeth. Bone grafting for implant preparation.

### Oral Appliances
Custom night guards for bruxism (teeth grinding), TMJ appliances, and sports mouth guards.

### Botox for TMJ and Bruxism
Therapeutic Botox injections for jaw tension, TMJ disorder, and chronic teeth clenching or grinding.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Is the Invisalign consultation really free?**
A: Yes. The initial Invisalign consultation includes an examination and digital scan at no charge.

**Q: What should I do if I have a dental emergency after hours?**
A: Contact the practice at (407) 877-9003. If you reach the virtual concierge, provide your details and the nature of your emergency. You will be flagged for priority follow-up at the earliest available time. If you experience spreading facial swelling, difficulty breathing, or high fever, go to the nearest emergency room.

**Q: Do you accept my insurance?**
A: The practice works with most major dental insurance providers. Call the office or ask the virtual concierge to help connect you with the team for specific plan verification.

**Q: How long does an Invisalign treatment take?**
A: Treatment duration depends on the complexity of your case. Typical cases range from 6 to 18 months. The doctor will provide a specific estimate during your consultation.

**Q: Can I get a crown in one visit?**
A: Yes. The practice uses CEREC same-day crown technology. In most cases, your crown is designed, milled, and placed in a single appointment.
