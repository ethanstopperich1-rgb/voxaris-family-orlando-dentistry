## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Do you accept my insurance?**
A: We accept most major dental insurance plans, especially PPO plans. Please call our office at (407) 877-9003 and we can verify your specific coverage before your visit.

**Q: How much does Invisalign cost?**
A: Invisalign pricing varies based on the complexity of your case. We offer free Invisalign consultations where we can provide you with a detailed treatment plan and cost estimate. Many insurance plans cover a portion of Invisalign treatment.

**Q: What should I do if I have a dental emergency?**
A: Call our office immediately at (407) 877-9003. We do our best to see emergency patients the same day. If it is after hours, leave a message and we will return your call as soon as possible. If you are experiencing severe bleeding or a life-threatening emergency, please call 911 or go to your nearest emergency room.

**Q: Do you see children?**
A: Yes! We love seeing patients of all ages, including young children. We recommend a child's first dental visit by their first birthday or when their first tooth comes in.

**Q: How often should I come in for a cleaning?**
A: Most patients should have a professional cleaning and exam every six months. Some patients with gum disease may need more frequent visits, typically every 3-4 months.

**Q: Do you offer sedation for anxious patients?**
A: We understand dental anxiety and provide a gentle, comfortable experience. Please discuss your concerns with our team and we will do everything we can to help you feel at ease.

**Q: What if I haven't been to the dentist in years?**
A: You are absolutely welcome here with no judgment. Many of our patients haven't been to the dentist in years, and we are here to help you get back on track with your oral health at your own pace.

**Q: Where are you located?**
A: We are located at 2704 Rew Circle, Suite 103, Ocoee, FL 34761. We are in the greater Orlando area, easily accessible from Ocoee, Winter Garden, Windermere, and surrounding communities.
